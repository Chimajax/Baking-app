Sponge Cake Recipe

Ingredients

  • 1 cup all-purpose flour
  • 1 cup granulated sugar
  • 4 large eggs, room temperature
  • 1 tsp vanilla extract
  • 1/4 cup unsalted butter, melted
  • 1/4 cup whole milk
  • 1 tsp baking powder
  • 1/4 tsp salt

Instructions

  1. Preheat your oven to 350°F (175°C). Grease and flour a 9-inch round cake pan.
  2. In a medium mixing bowl, sift together the flour, baking powder, and salt. Set aside.
  3. In a large mixing bowl, beat the eggs and sugar together until thick and pale, about 5-7 minutes.
  4. Add the vanilla extract to the egg mixture and mix until combined.
  5. Gently fold in the flour mixture until just combined.
  6. In a small bowl, mix the melted butter with the milk. Add this mixture to the batter and fold until just incorporated.
  7. Pour the batter into the prepared cake pan and smooth the top.
  8. Bake for 25-30 minutes, or until a toothpick inserted into the center comes out clean.
  9. Allow the cake to cool in the pan for 10 minutes, then turn it out onto a wire rack to cool completely.
  10. Frost and decorate as desired, then serve and enjoy!
