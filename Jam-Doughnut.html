Jam Doughnut

Ingredients

  • 2&1/2 cups all-purpose flour
  • 1/4 cup granulated sugar
  • 2 1/4 tsp active dry yeast
  • 1/2 tsp salt
  • 1/2 cup whole milk
  • 1/4 cup water
  • 1/4 cup unsalted butter, softened
  • 1 large egg
  • 1/2 cup jam (your choice of flavor)
  • Oil for frying
  • Extra granulated sugar for coating

Instructions

  1. In a large mixing bowl, combine 1 cup of flour, sugar, yeast, and salt.
  2. In a small saucepan, heat the milk, water, and butter until warm (not hot).
  3. Add the milk mixture to the flour mixture and beat until smooth.
  4. Add the egg and remaining flour, one cup at a time, to form a soft dough.
  5. Turn the dough onto a floured surface and knead until smooth and elastic, about 5-7 minutes.
  6. Place the dough in a greased bowl, cover, and let rise in a warm place until doubled in size, about 1 hour.
  7. Punch down the dough and turn it onto a lightly floured surface. Roll out to 1/2-inch thickness.
  8. Cut with a floured 2 1/2-inch round cutter. Place on greased baking sheets. Cover and let rise until doubled, about 30 minutes.
  9. In a deep fryer or large saucepan, heat oil to 375°F (190°C). Fry doughnuts, a few at a time, until golden brown on both sides. Drain on paper towels.
  10. When cool enough to handle, make a small hole in the side of each doughnut. Fill a pastry bag with jam and pipe it into each doughnut.
  11. Roll doughnuts in extra sugar to coat. Serve warm or at room temperature.
